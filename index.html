Memphish Grizzlies
Official Website

The Memphis Grizzlies are an American basketball team that are members of the National Basketball Association. The team is based in Memphis, Tennessee. Founded in 1995. The club plays its home games on the FedExForum since 2004.

About Us

When it was first formed, the team was located in the city of Vancouver, Canada under the name Vancouver Grizzlies as part of the NBA's expansion in the 1995-96 season. After the end of the 2000-01 season, the Grizzlies moved to the city of Memphis. Their uniforms are blue and yellow. This team has never won a division and league title in the NBA.
